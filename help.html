How You Can Help Reduce Light Pollution

Taking simple actions can make a big difference in reducing light pollution and preserving the night sky.

Use Outdoor Lighting Responsibly

Install outdoor lighting that is shielded and directed downward to avoid light trespass and glare. Consider using dimmers, motion sensors, or timers.

Advocate for Dark-Sky Friendly Policies

Encourage local government to adopt regulations that minimize light pollution, such as promoting dark-sky compliant streetlights and outdoor lighting.

Become an Advocate

Stay informed on how you can help reduce light pollution and get the latest news and tips.
